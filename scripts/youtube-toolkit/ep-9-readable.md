**Title:** Why Your Gut Health Affects Your Brain Function
**Content to clip:** The gut-brain connection is real and it's powerful. When your gut is inflamed or imbalanced, it directly affects your mood, your cognitive function, and your ability to think clearly. This is why healing chronic conditions requires looking at your digestive health first—it's not just about what you're eating, it's about how your body is processing everything.

### Short 3: Distance parenting actually works
**Title:** How Distance Parenting Can Actually Work
**Content to clip:** You can be an amazing mother even when you're not physically present 24/7. Distance parenting and co-parenting work when you're intentional about showing up fully for your children during the time you have together. It's about quality over quantity and making sure your kids know they're your priority even when work demands travel.

### Short 4: Small changes beat dramatic overhauls
**Title:** Why Small Health Changes Beat Big Overhauls
**Content to clip:** Small, consistent health changes create more impact than dramatic overhauls. Instead of completely changing your diet overnight, start with one meal. Instead of exercising for hours, start with 10 minutes a day. These small changes compound over time and create lasting transformation without overwhelming your system.
