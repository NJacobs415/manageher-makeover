### Short 2: Ethical fashion isn't just trendy
**Title:** What Ethical Fashion Really Means (It's Not Just Organic)
**Content to clip:** True ethical fashion means looking at the entire supply chain - from where materials come from, to how they're manufactured, to worker conditions. It's not just about organic cotton. It's about creating a system that respects people and planet at every single step.

### Short 3: Started at farmers markets
**Title:** How Farmers Markets Built My Fashion Empire
**Content to clip:** Before we had 3,000 retailers, I was testing my ethical fashion concept at local farmers markets. Starting small gave me direct customer feedback, helped me refine my products, and proved the concept without massive investment. Sometimes the humble beginning is the smartest strategy.
**Hashtags:** #SmallBusiness #EthicalFashion #Entrepreneur #StartupStory #BusinessGrowth #FarmersMarket #WomenInBusiness #Leadership

### Short 4: Your environment shapes leadership
**Title:** Why Your Location Affects Your Leadership Style
**Content to clip:** Living in alignment with your environment can completely change your creativity and leadership style. When I moved from the chaos of NYC to somewhere that matched my values, everything shifted - my decision making, my creativity, even how I led my team. Location matters more than we think.
