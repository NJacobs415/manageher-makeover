**Title:** Why motherhood is actually leadership training

### Short 2: Built a tech company by accident
**Title:** How to build a company without meaning to
**Content to clip:** Sometimes the best businesses come from solving problems you never intended to solve. I went from volunteer dance coach to tech CEO not because I had a grand plan, but because I saw a problem and couldn't ignore it. The most authentic businesses are born from real need, not strategy sessions.

### Short 3: Systems vs intuition is fake
**Title:** The systems vs intuition debate is wrong
**Content to clip:** You don't have to choose between systems and intuition - they work better together. The best leaders I know use data to inform their gut instincts and intuition to guide their systems. Stop thinking it's one or the other. You can be both strategic and intuitive.
**Hashtags:** #LeadershipStyle #DataDriven #IntuitiveLeadership #WomenInBusiness #LeadershipTips #BalancedLeadership #BusinessStrategy #FemaleLeaders

### Short 4: Start before you feel ready
**Title:** Why waiting until you're ready is a mistake
**Content to clip:** Starting before you feel ready is often the key to breakthrough success. I wasn't qualified to be a tech CEO on paper, but I was qualified by the problem I was solving. Waiting until you feel ready means waiting forever. Your experience matters more than your credentials.
