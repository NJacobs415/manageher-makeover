## 🎬 Shorts Ideas

### Short 1: Stop controlling your kids' journey
**Title:** The Passenger Seat Parenting Method That Actually Works
**Content to clip:** Parenting from the passenger seat means guiding your children without controlling their journey, allowing them to develop their own sense of purpose and direction. You're not the driver of their life—you're the guide helping them navigate.

### Short 2: Meaning beats happiness every time
**Title:** Why Meaning Matters More Than Happiness for Kids
**Content to clip:** Meaning is more essential than happiness for building resilience and mental health in children. When kids understand their 'why,' they can handle any challenge life throws at them. Stop chasing happiness and start building meaning.

### Short 3: Your kids NEED discomfort
**Title:** Why Protecting Your Kids Actually Hurts Them
**Content to clip:** Discomfort and challenges are necessary for building resilience—protecting children from all difficulties actually weakens them. When we shield our kids from every struggle, we rob them of the chance to build their resilience muscles.

**Title:** The Difference That Could Save Your Child's Mental Health
**Content to clip:** True belonging comes from authentic connection and shared values, not from fitting in or conforming to others' expectations. Teach your kids the difference—it could save their mental health and sense of self-worth.
